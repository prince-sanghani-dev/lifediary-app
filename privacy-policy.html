# Privacy Policy — LifeDiary **Last updated: May 2026** Prince Sanghani ("I", "me") built the **LifeDiary** app. This app is provided at no cost and is intended for personal use. By using LifeDiary, you agree to this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the app. --- ## 1. Information We Collect ### a) Data You Create All journal entries, tasks, habits, and finance records you create are stored **locally on your device**. This data belongs to you and is never accessed by me without your explicit action (e.g. enabling cloud backup). ### b) Google Account (Cloud Backup Only) If you choose to sign in for cloud backup, I collect your **Google account name and email address**. This is used solely to identify your backup in Firebase. It is never sold, shared, or used for marketing. ### c) Photos & Media Journal photos are stored **locally on your device**. If cloud backup is enabled, they are uploaded to your personal Firebase storage — accessible only to you. ### d) Advertising (AdMob) LifeDiary uses **Google AdMob** to display ads for free users. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads, subject to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy). Pro subscribers see no ads. ### e) Purchase & Subscription (RevenueCat) In-app purchases are processed via **RevenueCat** and **Google Play Billing**. RevenueCat may collect your purchase history and device information to validate entitlements. See [RevenueCat's Privacy Policy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy). I do not store your payment details. ### f) Crash & Analytics (Firebase) I use **Firebase Crashlytics** and **Firebase Analytics** to understand app stability and usage patterns. This data is anonymized and used only to improve the app. No personally identifiable information is included. --- ## 2. Local Storage The majority of your data — journals, tasks, habits, finance records, and settings — is stored **only on your device** using local storage. I have no access to this data unless you enable cloud backup. --- ## 3. Cloud Backup Cloud backup is **optional and off by default**. When enabled: - Your data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and stored in **Firebase Firestore and Firebase Storage** - Only you can access your backup, authenticated via your Google account - You can delete your backup at any time from the app settings --- ## 4. Notifications LifeDiary may send **local notifications** for reminders (habits, journal, tasks). These are scheduled entirely on-device. No notification data is sent to any server. --- ## 5. Children's Privacy LifeDiary is not directed at children under 13. I do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal data, please contact me and I will delete it promptly. --- ## 6.
